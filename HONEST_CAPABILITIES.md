## ‚ùå Things We CANNOT Do (Don't Promise)

### 1. Read Drawings Visually
- Cannot look at a PDF image and read "450x450mm"
- Cannot extract specs from drawing unless it's searchable text
- Cannot compare two drawings and find visual differences

### 2. Extract Text from Scanned PDFs
- Most construction drawings are images inside PDFs
- We store them, we can't read what's in them

### 3. Automatic Spec Extraction
- Cannot auto-populate "Column C1 = 450mm" from drawing
- Only know what users TELL us or what was in RFI responses

### 4. Photo Analysis
- Cannot look at site photo and detect issues
- Cannot compare photo to drawing
- We store photos with captions, that's it

### 5. Integrations with Other Software
- No Primavera/MS Project sync
- No SAP integration
- No automatic import from anywhere
