## THE PROBLEM (Tell This Story)

> "Imagine this real scenario:
>
> **Monday 10 AM:** Site engineer Ramesh needs to know the column size at Grid B2. He sends a message in the WhatsApp group.
>
> **Monday 11 AM:** Office person Priya sees the message, but she's in a meeting. She'll reply later.
>
> **Monday 2 PM:** Priya searches for the drawing. Finds STR-05. Sends photo of the page.
>
> **Monday 3 PM:** Ramesh sees it. But wait - is this the latest version? He's not sure. He calls the consultant.
>
> **Monday 4 PM:** Consultant confirms. Work finally starts.
>
> **Result: 6 hours lost. Work stopped. Everyone interrupted.**
>
> Now multiply this by 10 questions per day, across 5 projects. That's 50 interruptions. Every single day."
